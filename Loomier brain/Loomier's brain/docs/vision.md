# Vision

LOOMIER should become a creative operating system for building small-to-medium games with AI assistance.

---

## Product Feeling

The product should feel:

- fast
- playful
- direct
- editable
- creator-owned
- experimental without being chaotic

The creator should always feel that the game is theirs, not something the AI locked away.

---

## The Promise

```txt
Describe an idea.
Answer a few useful questions.
Get a playable game.
Edit it.
Understand it.
Export it.
```

---

## Audience

Primary:

- non-technical creators
- game jam creators
- students
- hobbyists
- designers who want playable prototypes

Later:

- indie teams
- educators
- asset creators
- template/system builders

---

## Differentiation

LOOMIER should compete on:

- editable structured output
- asset-aware generation
- hybrid 2D/3D runtime
- fast iteration
- clear creator control

Not on:

- "look, AI made a game once"
- generic app-builder dashboard UI
- huge agent architecture screenshots

---

## Product North Star

Time from idea to first playable preview should feel shockingly short.

Time from first preview to a better version should feel even shorter.
